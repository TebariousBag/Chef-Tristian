Welcome to Simple Cooking with Chef Tristian

Chef Tristian emerges as a luminary in the culinary firmament, his approach to cooking embodying a profound paradox - achieving sublime complexity through deliberate simplicity. His mastery lies not in elaborate preparations or rare ingredients, but in the precise calibration of fundamental elements that elevate basic dishes to transcendent heights.

Salmon Ramen Noodles

Each bite combines tender salmon, springy noodles, and savory soy sauce in perfect harmony, enhanced by the subtle richness of melted butter. I drain all the water to make sure the noodles are nice and sticky. Dinner for two for about $3. This is THE perfect dish for that special someone you love!

Mashed Potato Salmon Sandwich

Perfectly pan-seared salmon nestled between 2 slices of recently purchased Hawaiian bread, toasted to perfection, tied together with creamy mashed potatoes, and a drizzle of soy sauce - a surprisingly elegant sandwich that turns simple ingredients into something special.

Breakfast Dawgs

Experience the symphony of sensations as crispy bacon gives way to Chef Tristian's signature scrambled eggs, a premium cheddar bratwurst microwaved in a bowl of water, all resting upon butter-kissed toast - finished with the surprising grace of grape jelly.

Who is this Chef?

Chef Tristian was born and raised in Tulsa, Oklahoma. He continues to push the boundaries of culinary innovation while remaining grounded in his core philosophy: that great cooking should be accessible to everyone, regardless of skill level or economic means. His kitchen serves as a laboratory for creativity, where traditional techniques meet modern sensibilities, and where every dish tells a story of transformation and possibility. Through his work, he inspires a new generation of cooks to view familiar ingredients with fresh eyes and to understand that true culinary excellence resides in simplicity, respect for ingredients, and dedication to craft.
