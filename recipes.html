Recipes you can make at home!

Salmon Ramen Noodles

Each bite combines tender salmon, springy noodles, and savory soy sauce in perfect harmony, enhanced by the subtle richness of melted butter. I drain all the water to make sure the noodles are nice and sticky. Dinner for two for about $3. This is THE perfect dish for that special someone you love!
Mashed Potato Salmon Sandwich

Perfectly pan-seared salmon nestled between 2 slices of recently purchased Hawaiian bread, toasted to perfection, tied together with creamy instant mashed potatoes, and a drizzle of soy sauce - a surprisingly elegant sandwich that turns simple ingredients into something special.
Breakfast Dawgs

Experience the symphony of sensations as crispy bacon gives way to Chef Tristian's signature scrambled eggs, a premium cheddar bratwurst microwaved in a bowl of water, all resting upon butter-kissed toast - finished with the surprising grace of grape jelly.
Island Oasis

The circular macaroni formation creates a natural barrier, establishing a dramatic visual separation between the pasta "continent" and the savory bean soup "ocean." When you need something quick and cheap to make, this is my go to! It can be a lunch for two, or you can keep it all to yourself!

Salmon Ramen Noodles

Behold, dear culinary enthusiast, a masterpiece of modern gastronomy that transcends the boundaries of traditional cooking while embracing the elegant simplicity of contemporary convenience cuisine. This ingenious creation represents a harmonious fusion of East Asian flavors and Western comfort food sensibilities, elevated to an unprecedented level of sophistication through the thoughtful combination of carefully selected ingredients.

Ramen Gets Fancy Pants!

This dish presents itself as a masterfully composed symphony of textures and temperatures, beginning with the foundation of perfectly cooked ramen noodles. These slender strands, crafted through advanced dehydration technology, rehydrate with remarkable consistency, providing a satisfying chew that serves as the perfect canvas for your culinary artistry. Atop this carbohydrate foundation rests the pièce de résistance – succulent salmon fillets, preserved in their convenient pouch packaging. This marvel of modern food science delivers pristine fish protein with minimal preparation, its delicate flesh maintaining moisture and tenderness throughout the cooking process. The pouch format, far from being merely utilitarian, serves as a controlled environment that enhances flavor penetration while ensuring consistent doneness.

Instant Noodles Just Got a Promotion!

This dish represents more than mere sustenance. It embodies the spirit of modern culinary innovation. By embracing convenience foods while applying sophisticated cooking techniques, Chef Tristian has created a gastronomic experience that bridges traditional cooking methods with contemporary practicality. It stands as a testament to human ingenuity in transforming readily available ingredients into something truly exceptional.

Pro Tip

* After draining the water return the pot to the stove. Even though the burners are off it is still warm. You can add your butter, seasonings, soy sauce and salmon here and it will blend together easier. Plus it will help the smell permeate through your kitchen!

Pro Tip

* Make sure you set a timer for your noodles. These things are delicate, you wouldn't want to ruin your date night dinner with under or over cooked noodles!

Brat's All Folks! The Breakfast That's Gone to the Dogs

Jelly Good Time: The Breakfast That's Sticking Around

What sets Breakfast Dawgs apart is its masterful balance of technical precision and artistic vision.
